Manila American Cemetery and Memorial

The Manila American Cemetery and Memorial is a solemn and significant military cemetery located in Taguig City, Metro Manila, Philippines. Established in 1948, the cemetery serves as the final resting place for the fallen soldiers of the United States and its allies who fought in the Pacific theater during World War II. Situated on 152 acres, it is one of the largest American overseas military cemeteries.

Bonifacio Global City

Bonifacio Global City (BGC) is a modern and rapidly developing financial and lifestyle district in Metro Manila, Philippines. Formerly a military base, it has transformed into a bustling urban center known for its cosmopolitan atmosphere, high-rise buildings, green spaces, and a vibrant mix of residential, commercial, and recreational areas.

Venice Grand Canal Mall

Venice Grand Canal Mall is an upscale shopping and lifestyle destination situated within the McKinley Hill development in Taguig City, Metro Manila, Philippines. Inspired by the iconic Italian city of Venice, the mall replicates the romantic ambiance and architectural elements of Venice, complete with a man-made canal that runs through the center of the complex.

The Mind Museum

The Mind Museum is a world-class science museum located in Bonifacio Global City, Taguig, Metro Manila, Philippines. It's known for its interactive exhibits and engaging displays that cover various fields of science, technology, and natural history.

SM Aura Premier

SM Aura Premier is a upscale shopping mall located in Bonifacio Global City, Taguig, Metro Manila, Philippines. It is part of the SM Supermalls chain, known for its luxurious and contemporary design, offering a wide range of retail, dining, entertainment, and leisure options.

Philippine Army Museum Library and Archives

The Philippine Army Museum, Library, and Archives (PAMLA) is an institution that aims to preserve and showcase the history, heritage, and accomplishments of the Philippine Army. It serves as a repository of historical artifacts, documents, photographs, and memorabilia related to the army's rich history and contributions to the country.

Sta.Ana Church

The Saint Anne Parish Church, also known as Santa Ana Church or also known in its formal name as the Minor Basilica and Archdiocesan Shrine of St. Anne, is a Roman Catholic church and minor basilica located in Barangay Santa Ana, Taguig, Philippines. It is situated next to the Taguig River and across Plaza Quezon, where the statue of Manuel L. Quezon was erected when he was still serving as President of the Philippines. Saint Anne is the patroness of the church.
